# Weekly Recording Checklist

## Before Recording

- Pick the two winning formats from the previous week's metrics.
- Replace any placeholder viewer comment with a real comment.
- Prepare the exact distracting sites needed for each demo.
- Confirm Hotblock voice volume is clearly audible.
- Set the unlock challenge required for the script.
- Clean the Mac desktop and hide private tabs, notifications, and files.
- Record a short clean screen capture for each product action.
- Frame the face camera vertically with natural light and a quiet background.

## For Every Video

- Show conflict or payoff in the first second.
- Put `Mac app` in the opening overlay.
- Show Hotblock within the first two seconds.
- Record two opening-hook variations.
- Leave a short pause after the Hotblock voice line for the reaction.
- Capture one clean take and one looser, more irritated take.
- End with one CTA only: `Open hotblock.app on your Mac`.

## Editing

- Use a 1080 by 1920 vertical timeline.
- Keep most posts between 12 and 25 seconds.
- Add large burned-in captions.
- Keep important text outside the safe-zone overlay.
- Keep music below the app voice and narration.
- Remove pauses that do not improve the joke.
- Add `assets/final-card.svg` for the final 1.5 seconds.
- Export a clean version without a TikTok watermark for Reels and Shorts.

## Quality Check

- Understandable with sound muted.
- App voice clear with sound enabled.
- `Mac app` appears near the beginning.
- `hotblock.app` appears on the final frame and in the caption.
- No medical claims or claims that Hotblock is impossible to remove.
- No private information appears in screen recordings.
- Watch the final export once on a phone before posting.

## After Posting

- Record the post URL in `metrics.csv`.
- Reply to early comments for 20 to 30 minutes.
- Record 2-hour metrics.
- Record 48-hour metrics and the account median.
- Mark the next action as `Repeat`, `Recut`, `Reply video`, or `Retire`.
